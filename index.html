About me.

Hi, I'm a 22 year old software developer based in Southwest of England. My jouney began in the boredom of lockdown so I began learning how to program as a hobby.

From typing 'Hello world!' to delving into the new world of programming, a new found passion had begun. I was flung into a world defined by logic where the rules were often illogical. Where 0.1 + 0.2 != 0.3 and nothing is truly random.

Yet despite the frustration of the challenges, I still enjoyed overcoming and learning from them. I've since got my first role as a junior software developer.
As my time at this role is coming to an end, I'm now on the lookout for the next step to continue this journey!
